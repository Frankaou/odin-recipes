Cuban rice

You could be thinking, Why "Cuban" if it is spanish dish? Actually I do not know the reason. The point is that is very simple-making and actually delicious.

Ingredients

  • white rice
  • eggs
  • tomato sauce
  • banana
  • you can add whatever you think to go with

Steps

  1. put the rice for boil
  2. meanwhile make a fried eggs into a pan
  3. In the same pan add the tomato sauce with some vegetables
  4. when the rice is done, add the rice into the pan and mix them
  5. serve in a plate with banana garnish
