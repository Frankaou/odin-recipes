spanish omelette

This recipe is actually very famous dish across Spain.

It is very simple to make. Just involve using four ingredients

Now I am going to explain what are the ingredients and how you can make this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • sunflower oil
  • potato
  • eggs
  • salt

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them. Add some salt.
  2. Put a pan with the oil.
  3. When the oil is hot and the potatoes with care.
  4. Meanwhile whisk the eggs into a bowl.Add some salt.
  5. Check out when the potatoes are ready. You will realize when the potatoes are soft
  6. Get rid them of the pan and put them into a plate with paper towel.
  7. Mix the potatoes with the eggs within a bowl
  8. Put a pan with some oil and add the content from the bowl
  9. When one side is done turn the "tortilla" to the other side helping with a plate.
  10. Serve in a plate doing the step above.
