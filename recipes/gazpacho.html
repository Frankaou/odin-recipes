Gazpacho

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • tomato
  • onion
  • garlic
  • green pepper
  • cucumber
  • vinegar
  • salt

Steps

  1. cut all the vegetables into a bowl
  2. season all the vegetables with salt and vinegar
  3. using the blender crush the vegetables while add the oil
  4. serve in a bowl with some croutons and vegetables garnish
